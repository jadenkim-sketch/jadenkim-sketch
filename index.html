I camped there once and it was one of the best camping trips I could remember. Even though I have been wanting to go again there have been seasons where it was impossible to go and because it's so popular, it is always packed. Also, it is right next to Sequoia National Park so if you want to go to both it is possible.

Althought I have never been there and it is one of the most well known national parks, I still have an ongoing goal of going there at least one. To see the volcanic activity and geysers such as Old Faithful I have only seen in videos and photos. Even if I haven't been there it is a national park is worth for all people to go to.
